Score File

To store and load scores, we utilized XML to store the individual notes and effects within our score. Each note is tied to one instrument, and each instrument may be tied to one or more effects. We also allow any mixture of parallel or serial effects.

The score file for [Title of Selection Here] is shown below for reference.
